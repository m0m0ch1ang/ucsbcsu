Chinese Student Union

About Us

Chinese Student Union is a student-led organization at the UC Santa Barbara campus that aims to foster a safe and fun environment in which students can learn about and connect with Chinese culture and meet likeminded individuals. Members of CSU can find a home away from home and build their family at UCSB. Anyone interested in Chinese culture is welcome!
